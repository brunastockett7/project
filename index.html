Site Plan: Healthy Meal Planner

Site Name

Healthy Meal Planner

This name reflects the site's main goal: to provide tools and guidance for healthy and practical meal planning.

Site Purpose

This website helps users plan nutritious meals by providing recipe ideas, grocery lists, and a meal prep calendar. It empowers visitors to stay organized and eat healthy every day.

Scenarios

  • What are some healthy dinner options I can prep in under 30 minutes?
  • Is there a grocery list I can download for my meal plan?

Color Schema

We are using the following color scheme:

  • #8B0000 – Used for headings and buttons
  • #F7E7CE – Used for background sections and highlights

Typography

  • Playfair Display – Used for headings to give an elegant, classic look
  • Roboto – Used for body text for readability
